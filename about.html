About Us

Drug Drop is a premium subscription delivery service which caters to our customers using our very own FDA approved drugs. Our mission in this new venture is to provide an easy way for persons to obtain their prescription refills without having to wait in a long line at the pharmacy.

This auto-refill service also ensures that persons will never forget about getting their prescriptions refilled and subsequently never run out of their important meds. Drug Drop aims to provide exceptional service and timely delivery with a smile!
