Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Becoming a member with Drup Drop is simple and easy! Just click on the user icon in the top right hand corner of the page and enter the requested information on the signup page.

How long before I must renew my membership?

You can either choose to subscribe to our service yearly or monthly. This bill is charged to your account automatically until you choose to unsubscribe. Members may choose to cancel their subscription at any time but we'd hate to see you go!

What benefits do I get from becoming a member?

Members typically receive better delivery prices for non-prescription drugs as well as the ease of our auto-refill service for both prescription and non-prescription drugs.

Can I purchase non-prescription drugs without being a member?

We offer deliveries of non-prescription drugs in our shop no membership required, however our members receive better delivery fees along with the occasional discount!

Do I have to get a prescription from one of your doctors or can I use one from my own doctor?

Our doctors are highly skilled professionals, ready and willing to provide great care. However, if you have a doctor that you are already well acquainted with, we will accept their prescriptions too.
